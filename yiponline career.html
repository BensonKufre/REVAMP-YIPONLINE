    Technical Product Owner


    JOB SUMMARY

    The best candidate will most likely be a principled & curious people-oriented problem solver who possesses: Market Understanding -Be very good at prioritizing based on data and market intelligence Product Vision - Must optimize features to ensure the product solves the customers pain point People Skills - Must know how to negotiate with teams to get desired results. Experience as a Product Owner in a software as a service or e-commerce company

    REPORTS TO

    Product Manager

    JOB SKILLS

    Key Performance Indicators: Code quality | Sprint Velocity - story points / sprint | Customer Acceptance Score | Ship Dates Met

    JOB DESCRIPTION

    1. Define and prioritize the product backlog and vision for the product 2. Work closely with the development team to ensure successful delivery of the product 3. Act as the primary point of contact for all stakeholders, including customers, internal teams, and executive management 4. Understand market and customer needs and use that knowledge to drive the product direction 5. Continuously gather and incorporate feedback to improve the product 6. Ensure the product meets business and customer needs and is delivered on schedule 7. Collaborate with design, development, and QA teams to ensure high-quality delivery 8. Track and report on key metrics to measure the success of the product 9. Ability to think strategically and translate ideas into actionable plans 10. Strong analytical and problem-solving skills 11. Experience with product management tools such as Click UP , Jira and Asana 12. A customer-centric approach to product development 13. **Use Case Development & Training ** * Understand and document customer needs and translate them into requirements and user stories * Provide training & content on how to use the product. * Answer incoming questions about the product and its capabilities. 14. **Team Development & Intelligence Gathering** * Prepare & present Monthly Intelligence Reports (MIRs) - Assess emerging products and companies to measure their potential value or threat to the company and its products, as well as make recommendations on which new technologies to invest in or leverage. * Follow competitors and conduct capability and SWOT analysis every quarter * Recommend new features based on Customer request/trends/needs 15. **Documentation & Testing ** * Analyze and report on performance indicators, evaluate experiments * Develop Testing plans and define success criteria for testing and product acceptance. * Coordinate tests * Facilitate the creation and maintenance of proper product documentation. 16. Other duties as assigned.

    WHO WE ARE

    This is a learn tech company creating products to solve Africa’s e-commerce problems. We are led by a team of engineers and software developers who have worked for fortune 500 in Europe, the USA, and Canada. Hence we are bringing mature market learnings to solve local problems in Nigeria.

If you are looking for a place where you can contribute and be rewarded accordingly do apply.
